Oyo State, Nigeria

Oyo State, located in southwestern Nigeria, is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and strong agricultural economy. It is home to Ibadan, Nigeria’s largest city by landmass and a major center for education and commerce. The state is famous for its historical landmarks like the Cocoa House, Mapo Hall, and the Old Oyo National Park.
